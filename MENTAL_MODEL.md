# Portfolio Mental Model

This website is a premium portfolio for Dipta Brata Das.

It should feel like a hybrid of:

• a premium digital CV  
• a SaaS product landing page  
• a technical sales briefing  

The site must communicate:

• enterprise credibility  
• technical depth  
• strong communication skills  
• sales engineering expertise  

The goal is to demonstrate the ability to:

• explain complex systems
• guide enterprise evaluations
• run proof-of-concept processes
• bridge engineering teams and executives

---

# Narrative Flow

The homepage should guide the visitor through this narrative:

1. Who is this person?
2. What does he currently focus on?
3. What technology ecosystem does he understand?
4. What enterprise work has he done?
5. How does he approach technical sales?
6. How do enterprise deals evolve?
7. How does he guide evaluations?
8. What proof-of-concept work exists?
9. What principles guide his thinking?
10. Can he lead and communicate?
11. What insights does he share?
12. How can he be contacted?

---

# Design Philosophy

The visual design must feel:

• calm
• technical
• premium
• structured
• trustworthy

The design style should resemble a modern SaaS product.

Avoid:

• flashy animations
• loud gradients
• overly decorative visuals

Interactions should be subtle and purposeful.

---

# Target Impression

When a hiring manager finishes scrolling, they should think:

"This person understands both technology and enterprise software adoption."

The site should position Dipta Brata Das as a **high-level Sales Engineering leader**, not a freelancer or developer-for-hire.
